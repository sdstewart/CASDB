The Chinese Agricultural Scientist Database (CASDB) is a relational database that aims to document every Chinese agricultural scientists from the first half of the twentieth century. We compile biographical, education, and employment information from archival sources and personnel records that allows researchers to discover information about individual scientists or to conduct larger-scale statistical, spatial, or social network analyses.
